In order to complete the below steps, you need to be logged in. Log in to your account.

#Tutorial

  1. #Reviews

    Once you are logged in, go to your profile. Select Reviews.

  2. #Reviews About You

    On the "Reviews About You" tab, you will see all the reviews that users have left you. These reviews will be available on your profile for other users to see.

    Reviews cannot be changed or removed unless they violate our policy.

  3. #Reviews You Left

    On the "Reviews By You" tab and in the section Past reviews you've written. These reviews left by you will be visible on users profiles.

    Reviews cannot be changed or removed unless they violate our policy.

  4. #Outstanding Reviews

    If you have outstanding reviews that needs to be completed, they will appear in the list "Reviews to write". You will have 14 days to complete the review. If you haven't completed the review in time, you will no longer be able to leave a review.

    Shift'M will email you shortly after your Shift ended, to request you to write a review.
